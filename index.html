Web Design

Web design is the process of planning, conceptualizing, and arranging content intended for the internet. It focuses on the visual aesthetics, usability, and functionality of websites, encompassing layout, color schemes, typography, images, and interactive features. Web design also considers user experience (UX), responsive design for various screen sizes, and accessibility standards to ensure that websites are effective, user-friendly, and engaging across all devices. While traditionally centered around front-end development, modern web design often overlaps with aspects of coding and content strategy
